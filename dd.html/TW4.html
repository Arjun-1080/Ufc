A Trip to DUDHSAGAR



About dudhsagar

Dudhsagar Falls (lit. 'Sea of Milk') is a four-tiered waterfall located on the Mandovi River in the Indian state of Goa.
It is 60 km from Panaji by road and is located on the Belgaum–Vasco Da Gama rail route about 46 km east of Madgaon and 80 km south of Belgaum.
Dudhsagar Falls is amongst India's tallest waterfalls with a height of 310 m (1017 feet) and an average width of 30 metres (100 feet).

Travel details

        We board the train at 10pm from Belgavi RST to Londa, where we board another train at 2am heading to Goa.
        The train departs around 3am from Londa to goa, around 4am, train reaches Casterlock, after a trek of 10km from casterlock station, we reach Dudhsagar.
