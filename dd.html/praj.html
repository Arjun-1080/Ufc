SUNDI Falls



About sundi waterfall

The elegant Sundy waterfalls is located at Belgaum district. Falling from a height of about 50 feet, this crystal clear waterfalls offers a good picnic spot. As the water depth near the falls is less, it is safe to play in it. Sundi falls also offers a nice trekking destination along with Vaijnath and Mahipalgad.
It is 60 km from Panaji by road and is located on the Belgaumâ€“Vasco Da Gama rail route about 46 km east of Madgaon and 80 km south of Belgaum.
SUNDI Falls is amongst India's tallest waterfalls with a height of 310 m (1017 feet) and an average width of 30 metres (100 feet).
